The Deepfake Wiki Project

Where?

What?

The deepfake wiki is a project that aims to distill the research literature, and connected code, into a condensed structure where items are grouped by their conceptual content.
From this an at-a-glance dashboard infographic will be visible on deepfake.report which will convey the distilled take-aways of this collected information.
Such take-aways will include the quantity of literature that has been developed along each branch of the deepfake conceptual taxonomy, in each of the modalities; as well as the level to which defensive technologies have been developed in the research literature to combat each of these branches.

How?

We will be analysing by hand each item of deepfake literature and adding it to the wiki with all relevant metadata and external links.

Who?

This project has been created and is being maintained by Sergi Bray, a PhD candidate at University College London (UCL).
